Push
App that makes it easy for cash-strapped organizations to distribute news on cellphones.

Push is an open-source Android and iOS mobile app for news agencies and publications that donâ€™t have the time, money or resources to build their own custom news app. The app lets users view the publisher's latest stories and search for past articles using keywords. News organizations can also use the app to push content directly to their users' cellphone lock screens.

Push is compatible with multiple languages and includes the ability for news organizations to collect donations from users through PayPal.
