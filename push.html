Push
App that makes it easy for small organizations to distribute news on cellphones.
Introduction

Push is an open-source Android and iOS mobile app for news agencies and publications that donâ€™t have the time, money or resources to build their own custom code base for an app. The app lets users view the publisher's newest stories and search for keywords in old articles. News outlets also can use the app to push new content directly to their users' phone lock screens. Push is compatible with multiple languages and includes the ability for news organizations to collect donations from users through PayPal.
